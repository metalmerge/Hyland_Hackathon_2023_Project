In the game "Newton's Playground," you play as a small apple.
You'll need to navigate through a variety of environments, including forests and mountains, "Newton's Playground" is a fun and exciting game that educates young students on the physics that drive our world today.
Crashing through obstaces, exploring the physics of momentum, and learning about the Newtons laws through this game revolution.
